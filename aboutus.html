Our Journey

How TagoreTunes
Came to Be

🌱
2018
The Spark
Founder Ravi Krishnan, frustrated by the lack of quality music education in Indian cities, started TagoreTunes with 3 teachers and a borrowed studio in Bandra, Mumbai.
📱
2020
Going Digital
When the pandemic hit, we pivoted entirely to online. Instead of shrinking, we grew — expanding to 14 new cities in 8 months and tripling our student base.
🏆
2022
National Recognition
Won the "Best EdTech Platform — Arts & Culture" award at the India Education Summit. Crossed 2,000 active students and 60 teachers across India.
🌍
2025
Today & Beyond
4,800+ students, 120+ teachers, 18 instruments, 18 cities. We're now launching our first international cohort for Indian diaspora students worldwide.
Our Mission

Music for
Every Student,
Everywhere

We believe the gap between a student and a great music teacher should be zero — not geography, not income, not availability. That's the problem we're solving, one lesson at a time.

Every teacher on TagoreTunes is handpicked through a rigorous 3-stage audition and pedagogy review. We don't just hire musicians — we hire people who love to teach as much as they love to play.

✓ Rigorous 3-stage teacher audition process
✓ Background verification for every instructor
✓ Pedagogy training for all new faculty members
✓ Monthly quality reviews and student feedback loops

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

These aren't wall posters — they're the actual principles that guide how we hire teachers, design courses, and serve students every single day.

🎯
Excellence First

We'd rather have 100 extraordinary teachers than 1,000 average ones. Quality is non-negotiable at every touchpoint — from teacher auditions to platform design.

🌱
Accessible to All

Great music education shouldn't be a privilege. We offer EMI options, scholarships for deserving students, and a free first session for everyone — no exceptions.

🤝
Teacher-First Culture

Our teachers are our product. We pay fairly, treat respectfully, and invest in their growth. A happy teacher makes a great student — it's that simple.

💡
Always Improving

We read every review, act on every complaint, and ship improvements weekly. TagoreTunes in 2025 is dramatically better than TagoreTunes in 2018, and 2026 will be better still.

🎭
Celebrate the Stage

Learning without performing is incomplete. We host quarterly recitals, annual concerts, and open-mic events so every student gets their moment in the spotlight.

🌍
Cultural Roots

India's musical heritage is our greatest asset. From Carnatic ragas to Hindustani thaats, we honour classical traditions while embracing every modern style too.
